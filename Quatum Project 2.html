BB84 Polarization

The BB84 protocol is the foundation of Quantum Key Distribution. It utilizes two mutually unbiased bases for photon polarization to ensure security through the principle of quantum observer effect.

texture Polarization Bases

Rectilinear Basis (+) 0°, 90°

Encodes '0' as Horizontal and '1' as Vertical. Standard fiber-optic carrier default.

Diagonal Basis (X) 45°, 135°

Encodes '0' as 45° and '1' as 135°. Used to detect measurement interference.
